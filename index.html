Actuaries and STatisticians endeavour to design innovative, inclusive insurance products in a changing RISK landscape.

Within this project a team of researchers from KU Leuven and UCLouvain works along three main research lines:

Research line 1


STATISTICAL TOOLBOX

Clustering and regularization methods for high-dimensional multi-type insurance data

Dynamic risk prediction and prevention with a.o. cure and joint models

Research line 2


INCLUSIVE INSURANCE


To contribute to a more inclusive and fair access to insurance

Research line 3


PEER-TO-PEER

Novel, transparent and actuarially sound risk sharing rules

To study P2P insurance solutions and to design effective, integrated risk management solutions

The project is funded by the Excellence of Science (EOS) 2021 call by FWO and FNRS.
