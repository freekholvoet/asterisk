Current and previous positions

2009-present Teaching of various statistics courses at bachelor, master and PhD level at UCLouvain

Research interests

His research puts focus on mathematical and nonparametric statistics, survival analysis and medical statistics, inference using empirical likelihood, semiparametric and nonparametric mean and quantile regression, dependence modeling and copulas.

None
